The Future in Veterinary Technology

A peek into my imagination...

My Ideas

Everybody knows that technology is constantly being advanced and developed at extreme rates so I thought up of some possible vet tech for the future.

Medicine Delivery Drone

The idea of a medicine delivery drone was inspired by the autonomous delivery robots that are already used in some areas such as Wisconsin, Florida, Washington, and more. However, unlike the robots that drive on land, my drones would fly between destinations for faster deliveries.

Functions

  • The drones would use GPS to navigate between locations more efficiently by selecting the fastest and safest routes while also avoiding any obstacles, this also allows for farther distances.
  • To avoid the risk of running out of power mid deliver, the drones will be powered by eco-friendly solar panels.
  • There will an app where users connect with vets to order prescribed drugs from pharmacies to avoid the trouble of pick ups. Refills can also ordered after consulting with the vet.
  • Users will be able to track their delivery with quick on-the-spot GPS tracking on both the drone and their package.

Recovery Plan App

The idea of this app is to make the recovery of pets easier and safer by constantly providing status updates of the recovery progress to pet parents as well as the vets.

Function

  • The app will use the patient's health information, past medical history, and ailment information to procure a treatment plan that also predicts the recovery process.
  • Users can log any changes to the patient's condition and the app will read if the patient is healing is on track and supply feedback to the user.
  • If the patient's condition does not improve or worsens, the app will automatically notify the vet.

Destresser

The purpose of this machine is to automatically calm down stressed animals

Function

  • The machine will use sensors to detect the stress or agrivation levels of the animal.
  • Once stress levels exceed a certain amount the machine will automatically start emitting calming agents suitable for the specific animal.
  • This will help keep both humans and animals safe from harm.
